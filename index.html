ABOUT SPEAKEASY BROWNIE

We're five guys who love a good laugh and making music. We formed SpeakEasy Brownie in 2016, in Hyderabad, India.

We write happy songs. We also write serious songs to make sure we're not viewed as being carefree and superficial.
Image is everything, which is why we chose a chipmunk as our mascot. We could easily live on coffee, sponge cake and biryani. Water is needed from time-to-time. One of our dreams is to endorse Bangalore Iyengar Bakery in Secunderabad, India.

We have climbed over 1612 flights of stairs with heavy gear in tow, misunderstood 5,396 of each others' sentences, drank 16,666 tiny filter coffees and missed 2 phone calls while holed up in our practice pad.

Much of the information above is true. There's no denying this, though: you'll be glad you're reading all this and not having one of us read it out to you!

Inspired by artists and bands from a variety of genres, we draw from personal life experiences and stories to compose our music. Get to know SpeakEasy Brownie and listen to our music! Did you notice that the previous sentence didn't end with "now"? Our music is here to stay, so there's no rush, really. Enjoy! :)
