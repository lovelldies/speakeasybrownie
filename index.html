ABOUT SPEAKEASY BROWNIE

SpeakEasy Brownie is made up of 4 motor mouths who speak lightning-quick and 1 dreamer-pretender who pretends to understand what they're saying.

All. The. Time!

Formed in August 2015, they're a multi-genre band, currently gearing up for the release of 2 singles while working on writing original material for their first album due in March 2017. SpeakEasy Brownie draws heavily from a wide range of musical influences ranging from Western & Indian Classical, Gospel, Funk, Pop, Blues and Jazz to EDM, Classic Rock, Progressive Metal and Djent. The band's mantra is simple: make music that is inclusive, and fun to play and listen to!
