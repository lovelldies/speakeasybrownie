ABOUT SPEAKEASY BROWNIE

We, SpeakEasy Brownie, are a 5-person band from Hyderabad, India that was formed in August 2015.

We're all about making music that is inclusive and that evokes all sorts of emotions — joy, sorrow, passion, hope, conviction...

OK, mostly joy with a dash of the rest.

A typical SpeakEasy Brownie set usually features mostly original compositions by the band, with a few covers — often reworked and mashed up with other songs in a new, attractive skin. We take special delight in making audiences dance and sing along to our songs. Our day is made when audiences throw their inhibitions aside and join in the revelry with us!
